About

Applied scientist building large-scale conversational AI systems that transform unstructured conversations into actionable intelligence.

I design and deploy production-grade machine learning systems across multilingual speech, NLP, and generative AI. My work spans real-time end-to-end speech translation, privacy-preserving PII redaction across global languages, semantic retrieval over high-volume conversational data, and LLM-powered pipelines for automated theme and trend detection.

Beyond model performance, I focus on robustness, scalability, and measurable business impact—optimizing latency-accuracy tradeoffs, improving multilingual generalization, and ensuring privacy-first AI systems that operate reliably at production scale.

I care deeply about building AI systems that are both technically rigorous and practically impactful—bridging research innovation with real-world deployment to enable organizations to better understand and serve their customers.

Recent focus areas

Automatic Speech Translation

Real-time end-to-end speech translation (low latency, high quality)

  • Designed RL-based training using Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO).
  • Optimized latency–accuracy tradeoff with contextual signals.
  • Planned multilingual expansion to 10+ languages (PFIGS, CJK + Cantonese, Taiwanese) with bidirectional support.

Impact: Enables natural real-time cross-language communication in multilingual call centers, healthcare, and emergency services.

PII Redaction

Multilingual PII redaction for conversation analytics

  • Generated synthetic training datasets with LLMs across 14 languages.
  • Expanded language + locale support (incl. 6 English locale variants).
  • Retrained XLM-R on expanded multilingual data; improved code-switching detection.
  • Language-optimized tokenization: sentence-level (EN/EU) and character-level (CJK).

Impact: Protects sensitive data (e.g., bank/credit card) across global customers and strengthens privacy guarantees.

Trend / Theme Detection

Generative AI pipeline for automated trend detection in Amazon Connect

  • Built end-to-end multi-day, multi-split processing pipeline.
  • Integrated LLMs for issue extraction and theme generation.
  • Defined clustering + generation metrics; tested across finance/retail/hospitality.
  • Expanded multilingual support to French and Spanish; collaborated to deploy to production.

Impact: Helps thousands of customers identify emerging themes and contact drivers at scale.

Semantic Matching

Semantic search over thousands of conversations

  • Unified production model: all-distilroberta-v1 with token-max pooling.
  • 70%+ precision across multiple domains/datasets with calibrated thresholds.
  • Explored pooling strategies and domain-specific improvements to reduce false positives.

Impact: Enables meaning-based search beyond keyword matching for voice + chat analytics.

KG + LMs

Hybrid KGQA + LM reasoning to reduce hallucinations

  • Integrated ReFinED entity linking (Wikipedia/Wikidata, multilingual).
  • Improved question understanding via filtering and candidate disambiguation.
  • Trained zero-shot question generation in 5 languages using Wikidata templates.

Impact: Fact-grounded answers with large performance gains vs LMs alone on complex reasoning tasks.

Research

Material property prediction (tabular ML)

  • Built dataset from MatWeb (17,052 metal materials) and automated preprocessing pipeline.
  • BERT-style model adapted for tabular prediction (reported MSE 2.98).
  • Tested GPT/Claude prompting approaches for property prediction.

Impact: Reduces need for costly experiments by predicting missing properties reliably.
